01 — About

Why I build the way I do.

I'm Samanvay, an aspiring AI Backend Engineer with a long-term goal of building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

My curiosity started with AI, but it quickly expanded beyond language models. I wanted to understand everything that makes an AI application work—from APIs and authentication to databases, automation, and the backend systems that allow intelligence to operate reliably at scale. That curiosity is what led me to backend engineering.

Today, I'm building a strong foundation in backend engineering through Python, Flask, databases, authentication, and production-ready APIs before moving deeper into AI engineering. My long-term goal is to build my own Jarvis-like AI assistant and help startups scale by creating intelligent systems that automate work, solve real problems, and turn ideas into reliable products. Every project in this portfolio is another step toward that vision.
